James Wenz

Professional Studio Drummer

James Wenz

Hi!!!! My name is James, and I'm a drummer. Wee!!! I sure do love drumming. I drum every day for at least four minutes to make sure I can do all my rudiments, such as the falafel waffle and the poopity scoop. I started drumming when I was but a wee lad, and ever since, I just love to retreat to my world of loud banging and clashing. It is where I feel truly at peace with the world, a world of sound and music that I can create. Hot dog!

And now it's time for me to share my sweet drumming with the world! So greetings, friends, family, employers . If you want to pay me for my drumming, I'm not stopping you.
